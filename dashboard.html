Meta CAPI Guide

Step 1: Use the Qualified/Unqualified buttons on each lead card. HVAC business owners = Qualified.
Step 2: Click "Send CAPI" to push qualified, booked, and won leads to Meta as conversion events.
Step 3: Meta receives: Qualified → Lead, Booked → Schedule, Won → Purchase, Unqualified → Bad.
Step 4: In Ads Manager, switch optimization to "Conversion Leads." Meta optimizes for leads you actually care about.
Why This Matters: Mark your leads to generate data, then Meta will stop showing ads to landscapers, restaurants, and handymen.
